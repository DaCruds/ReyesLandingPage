About Us

Bringing You the Best NIght of your Life

At BOSS UWA BAR & RESTO, we believe that a memorable dining experience is not just about delicious food; it's about creating an atmosphere that celebrates the joy of life. Nestled in the heart of Lucena City, our resto bar offers a unique fusion of delectable cuisine, refreshing drinks, and live entertainment, making it the perfect destination for food enthusiasts and entertainment seekers alike.

Culinary Excellence: Our culinary team, led by seasoned chefs with a passion for flavors, takes great pride in crafting a diverse menu that caters to every palate. From savory starters to mouthwatering mains and delectable desserts, each dish is carefully curated using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients. Whether you crave traditional comfort food or daring culinary innovations, our menu promises to tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Thirst-Quenching Beverages: No exceptional dining experience is complete without a carefully curated selection of beverages. At BOSS UWA BAR & RESTO, our bartenders are skilled mixologists who concoct an array of handcrafted cocktails, premium spirits, fine wines, and craft beers to complement your meal. Whether you're in the mood for a classic concoction or an innovative signature drink, we have something to please every connoisseur.

Sensational Entertainment: As the sun sets and the night comes alive, so does our resto bar. We pride ourselves on hosting top-notch live entertainment that adds a spark to your dining experience. From live bands playing your favorite tunes to talented artists showcasing their skills, our entertainment lineup is sure to make your visit an unforgettable one.
